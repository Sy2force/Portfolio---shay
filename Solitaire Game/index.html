Solitaire Game Help

How to Play

The goal of solitaire is to move all cards to the foundations (top right), sorted by suit in ascending order (from Ace to King).

How to Move Cards

  1. Select a card by clicking on it
  2. Move it by clicking on the destination

Movement Rules

Source Destination Rules
Deck Waste Click on the deck to flip a card
Waste Foundation Ace, then ascending order of the same suit
Waste Tableau Descending order, alternating colors
Tableau Foundation Ace, then ascending order of the same suit
Tableau Tableau Descending order, alternating colors
Foundation Tableau Descending order, alternating colors

Tips

  • Try to free Kings to place them on empty columns
  • Uncover face-down cards as soon as possible
  • Don't rush to place cards on foundations if you need them elsewhere

Beginner Shortcuts

Situation Recommended Action
Available Ace Place it on a foundation
Face-down card Priority: uncover this card
Available King Place it on an empty column
Empty deck Click on it to recycle the waste pile
