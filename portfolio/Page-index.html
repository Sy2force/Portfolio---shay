About Me

My Passion for Digital: What I love most about being a Digital Strategist & Designer is creating solutions that make a difference. My unique journey, which took me from Paris to Israel at age 15, taught me to see challenges as opportunities. My varied experiences - from the army to cooking, then to digital - have given me a unique way of thinking and creating.

My Approach: I enjoy taking complex ideas and transforming them into concrete projects that speak to people. My military experience taught me organization, cooking gave me attention to detail, and today, I put these qualities to work in digital design and marketing. I'm always on the lookout for new trends to create designs that stand out.

My Vision: For me, good design must be both beautiful and effective. I don't just settle for making things look pretty - I create digital solutions that have real impact. Whether it's for a website, marketing campaign, or digital strategy, my goal is simple: make every project not only visually attractive but also truly useful for those who use it.
